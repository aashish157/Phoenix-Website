About

Every year at LNMIIT the iconic pink brick walls wait to tell you a story… A story of how we set it all lose for 3 days and bring fun, creativity and life to the doorstep of over 10000 people! Touted as one of the best cultural festivals in Rajasthan, Vivacity sure lives up to its name.

Inaugurated in the (2006 – 2007) season, the 7th edition of Vivacity, is dedicated to the fantasy world we wish we could live in.It has showcased some of the biggest names in the Indian entertainment industry – including Javed Ali, Mohit Chauhan, Euphoria

The event features a gamut of cultural and fine arts events, ranging from choreographed dance displays and fashion parades to numerous exciting technical & management games to tickle the wits of the best and the brightest who visit the campus. Plays, pop and rock shows, fusion and fashion shows,and Mr and Ms Vivacity ensure that the atmosphere is always electric and there’s never a dull moment.
